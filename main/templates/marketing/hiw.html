How RallyNex Works

Getting Started

1
Create Your Profile
Complete your profile with bio, contact info, education details and profile picture. Verify your account by submitting ID documents for trusted status.
2
Find People to Follow
Use suggested users to find friends with similar interests. Following users lets you see their campaigns in your feed.

Creating Campaigns

3
Start a Campaign
Click upload to create campaigns with titles, descriptions, media (images/audio). Set visibility (public or private) and optional funding targets/duration.
4
Manage Your Campaign
Add regular updates through Activities. View donations, comments, and engagement metrics. Edit campaign details anytime from your profile.

Supporting Causes

5
Engage with Campaigns
Love campaigns to show support, comment with your thoughts, and join campaigns to become an active participant and view their activities regularly.
6
Participation Options
Donate financially (anonymous option available), brainstorm ideas to help the cause, or purchase campaign products (when available).

Communication

7
Chat with Others
Start private chats with individuals or groups. Shared links automatically become clickable. Get notifications for new messages.
8
Get Notified
Receive alerts for new followers, campaign interactions (loves/comments), donation receipts, and chat messages. All notifications can be marked as read.

Discover Content

9
Explore Campaigns
Browse by categories or use search. See what campaigns your friends support. Mark "Not Interested" to refine suggestions in campaign details.
10
Private Campaigns
View campaigns shared specifically with you in a separate section. Manage who can see your private campaigns.

Your Profile

11
Manage Your Account
Edit personal information anytime. View your campaign history and verification status. See your total impact through loves received and supporter counts.
12
Affiliate Links
Discover recommended resources. Support campaigns through affiliate purchases that contribute to causes you care about.

Recognition & Rewards

13
Earn Changemaker Awards
Get recognized for your contributions! Our system automatically awards Bronze, Silver, and Gold badges based on your campaign participation and impact:
  • Bronze: For starting your first campaign
  • Silver: After creating 2 successful campaigns
  • Gold: For launching 3+ impactful campaigns
14
Become a Top Participant
Your contributions to campaigns (donations, brainstorming, purchases) are tracked. The most active supporters appear as top participants on campaign pages, gaining visibility in our community.
15
Community Leader Status
Users who consistently provide value to the community receive special recognition. This includes verified badges, profile highlights, and early access to new features.

Start Your Own Campaign!

Do you have a cause you're passionate about? Join Rallynex and create your own impactful campaign today!
